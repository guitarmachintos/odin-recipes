Fried Rice Recipe

Description

An easy and simple fried recipe which is good for making use of those leftover Ingredients in your fridge.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups leftover white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cloves of shallot
  • 2 sprig spring onion
  • 1tbsp neutral oil
  • 1tbsp soy sauce
  • 1tbsp white pepper
  • 1tbsp MSG
  • 1cup of any leftover meat

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the garlic amd shallot into tiny pieces
  2. Wash and cut the spring onion
  3. Rinse the wok with water then heat it until completely dry.
  4. Pour the oil and season the wok by tilting the oil to cover the wok.
  5. Add the garlic and shallot into the wok.
  6. Add HALF of the spring onion into the wok.
  7. After the garlic and shallot are starting to turn brown, add the leftover meat and stir
  8. Crack the eggs and add them into the wok. If you want the whites and yolk to be separated, wait a bit before stirring.
  9. Add the rice into the wok.
  10. Punch the rice with your ladle or spatula to make sure there is no clump left.
  11. Pour the soy sauce onto the edge of the wok (not on top of the rice).
  12. Add the white pepper and MSG.
  13. Keep stirring and flipping until you starts to see a bit of smoke (rice starting to get charred).
  14. Add the spring onion and stir for a bit, and quickly transfer to a plate to avoid the spring onion wilting.
  15. If you have it, sprinkle some fried shallots and add prawn cracker to add more texture and flavour.
  16. Enjoy.
