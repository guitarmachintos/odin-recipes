Oatmeal Recipe

Description

A convenient oatmeal recipe which is healthy, high protein, and not that bad.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup instant oatmeal
  • 200ml of plain milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tbsp non-chunky peanut butter
  • 1 cup protein powder
  • 1 cup berries
  • 1 cup clusters

Steps

  1. Add the oatmeal into a bowl.
  2. Pour the chia seeds evenly.
  3. Add the milk until the oatmeal and chia seeds are completely submerged.
  4. Microwave at 800W for 1 minute and 10 seconds.
  5. Using two separate spoons, scoop the honey and peanut butter from the container. Add them into the oatmeal.
  6. Add the protein powder into the bowl.
  7. Stir the oatmeal very well until you can't see the honey, peanut butter, and the protein powder. Make sure nothing sticks in each spoon.
  8. Add the toppings (clusters is very good for enhancing the texture).
